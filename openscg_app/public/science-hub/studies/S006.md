# Dehkordi 2019: SCG for CAD Screening

> **Quick Conclusion:** Examines the diagnostic performance of SCG in CAD screening, observing results comparable to stress-echocardiography in this cohort.

---

### 📊 Key Accuracy Metrics

| Metric | Result |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Rest Model Accuracy** | 74% (Sensitivity 75%, Specificity 72%) |
| **Exercise Model Accuracy** | 81% (Sensitivity 82%, Specificity 84%) |
| **Exercise SCG AUC** | 0.91 |
| **ECG Stress Test Accuracy** | 65% (in the same cohort) |

### 🔍 Study Analysis

#### **Objective & Population**
Observational / Validation Study with Exercise Stress Test. Cohort: 180 individuals (117 with significant CAD >=50% stenosis, 63 controls) (N=180).

#### **What it Supports**
Reports 81% diagnostic accuracy for exercise SCG in detecting obstructive CAD (>=50% stenosis) compared to angiography.

#### **What it Does Not Support**
The study does not support the localization of stenosis (where the blockage is), but rather its presence and severity. It is not a complete replacement for invasive angiography but a powerful gatekeeper.
