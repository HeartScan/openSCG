### 🔍 Study Analysis

#### **Objective & Population**
Open-access Dataset / Remote Monitoring Study. Cohort: 108 volunteers (46 male, 61 female), Age 18-62 (N=108).

#### **What it Supports**
Provides a large-scale real-world SCG dataset for technical characterization.

#### **What it Does Not Support**
This study does not provide diagnostic validation for heart disease but provides the raw material needed to develop such diagnostics.
