# Dehkordi 2019: Multimodal Echo Investigation

> **Quick Conclusion:** Establishes a quantitative comparison between SCG and ICG, suggesting higher accuracy for SCG in estimating systolic time intervals against ultrasound references.

### 🔍 Study Analysis

#### **Objective & Population**
Cross-sectional / Validation Study. Cohort: Healthy adults (N=86).

#### **What it Supports**
Reports that 86-90% of SCG-derived timings match multimodal echocardiography ranges, outperforming traditional impedance cardiography (ICG).

#### **What it Does Not Support**
The study does not demonstrate SCG performance in patients with cardiovascular diseases or in real-world (out-of-lab) conditions.
