# Parlato 2025: FOSTER Multimodal Dataset

> **Quick Conclusion:** S045 (FOSTER) is a high-quality 2025 dataset that represents the future of multimodal 'Force-based' monitoring. For OpenSCG, it provides the precise temporal alignment needed to train models that combine vibrations (SCG) with acoustic data (PCG), enabling more robust detection of heart valve events.

---

### 📊 Key Accuracy Metrics

| Metric | Result |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Total Data** | 7 minutes per subject @ 10 kHz sampling rate |
| **SCG SNR** | 83.73 ± 0.53 dB |
| **IBI Accuracy (dHF-FCG vs ECG)** | R2 > 0.99, Bias 0.0 ms, LoA ~±6 ms |
| **Repository** | osf.io/3u6yb |

### 🔍 Study Analysis

#### **Objective & Population**
Dataset Description / Technical Validation Study. Cohort: 40 healthy subjects (20 males, 20 females), Age 26.93 ± 7.09 (N=40).

#### **What it Supports**
The study supports the integration of multiple mechanical sensing modalities (SCG, FCG, PCG) for a holistic view of heart function. It proves that mechanical sensors can track heart and breathing rates with high precision (unit slopes vs ECG) and provides a unique high-frequency (10 kHz) dataset for developing advanced AI algorithms.

#### **What it Does Not Support**
This study does not provide diagnostic evidence for heart failure or other pathologies, as the cohort consisted of young, healthy volunteers.
