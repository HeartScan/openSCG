### 🔍 Study Analysis

#### **Objective & Population**
Comprehensive Literature Review. Cohort: General population / Research subjects across multiple studies (N=None).

#### **What it Supports**
The review supports the technical validity of using modern MEMS accelerometers for heart vibration sensing. It validates the transition of SCG from lab-only experiments to real-world applications in home and field monitoring.

#### **What it Does Not Support**
This review does not support specific diagnostic accuracy claims for a single device, but rather provides the scientific framework for the entire field of cardiomechanical sensing.
