### 🔍 Study Analysis

#### **Objective & Population**
Comprehensive Narrative Review. Cohort: Review of 80+ studies (N=None).

#### **What it Supports**
The review supports the scientific legitimacy of SCG for monitoring heart rate variability, systolic time intervals, and detecting cardiovascular diseases. It validates that modern signal processing and AI can overcome historical noise issues, making SCG suitable for wearable and 'at-home' medical technology.

#### **What it Does Not Support**
This review does not support the effectiveness of any specific consumer app or device, but provides the peer-reviewed evidence for the entire technology class.
